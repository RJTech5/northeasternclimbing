Event Information

First time attending a Northeastern Climbing Club event? Below is more information about our events so you can get a better idea of what to expect!

Northeastern Recreational Climbing Club members meet regularly 4 times a week, at Central Rock Fenway and Rock Spot South Boston, during the fall and spring semesters. These meetings offer a chance for climbers to get to know each other and improve their climbing skills. Most meetings are unstructured, but occasional group exercises and training are offered. Check out our meetings page for more information!

NRC regularly hosts outdoor climbing events, which are a great way to get introduced to outdoor climbing. These events offer bouldering and top rope opportunities to climbers of all levels. No experience is required to participate in these outings!
